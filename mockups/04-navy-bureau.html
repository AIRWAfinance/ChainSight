§II · Methodology

Seven typologies.
Citation per finding. Zero black box.

Each detector is a short, reviewable function — not a model. Results are reproducible across runs, and every finding is tied back to a specific regulatory source.

I. Sanctions exposure

Direct counterparty screening

Flags any transfer where the counterparty appears on OFAC's SDN list or sanctions registries from EU, UK, UN.

OFAC SDN · daily sync
II. Mixer exposure

Privacy-tool flow tracing

Detects deposits to or withdrawals from known mixers — Tornado Cash, Sinbad, Wasabi — with value-weighted severity.

FATF RFI §3.1(b)
III. Scam exposure

Curated cluster matching

Cross-references against known scam, drainer, ransomware, and rug-pull clusters with categorical severity.

FATF RFI §4.1
IV. Layering

Pass-through pattern

Identifies inbound flows forwarded out within 60 minutes in near-identical amounts — classic placement-layering signature.

FATF RFI §4.2(a)
V. Peel chain

Sequential decreasing outflows

Detects long chains of small outflows from a high-value source — a hallmark of structured crypto laundering.

FATF RFI §4.2(c)
